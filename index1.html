What is Cognitive Science?
Cognitive science is the scientific study of the mind, brain, and intelligence, particularly as it relates to mental abilities such as reasoning, decision making, memory, learning, attention, language, perception, and motor control.


Why Cognitive Science?
Cognitive science is the scientific study of the human mind. It is a highly interdisciplinary field, combining ideas and methods from psychology, computer science, linguistics, philosophy, and neuroscience. The broad goal of cognitive science is to characterize the nature of human knowledge – its forms and content – and how that knowledge is used, processed, and acquired. One of the major reasons of development of Cognitive Science is that it helps us in understanding the behaviour and emotions of life. It covers many fields like consiousness, stimulus and response of the unconsious brain.
